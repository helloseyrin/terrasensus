## Design Principles
- **Farmers in the field**: large touch targets, high contrast, readable in sunlight
- **Data-dense but calm**: lots of numbers, but never overwhelming — use colour sparingly for status (green/amber/red)
- **Lightning fast feel**: optimistic UI updates, skeleton loaders, no spinners for common actions

## Farmer Persona (self-tested)
User: farmer in the field, iPhone, outdoor conditions, checking soil readings between tasks.
Key flows to validate in prototype:
1. Open app → see current field health at a glance (single screen)
2. Tap alert → understand problem + recommended action in under 10 seconds
3. Upload a lab report photo → confirm it was processed correctly
4. View AI fertiliser recommendation → understand eco-score + supplier suggestion
